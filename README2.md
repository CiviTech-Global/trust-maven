# TrustMaven

TrustMaven is a comprehensive risk management platform that empowers organizations to identify, assess, treat, and continuously monitor risks across all domains. From financial and cybersecurity risks to operational, regulatory, reputational, and even AI‑specific risks, TrustMaven provides an integrated suite of tools that ensures your organization remains resilient, compliant, and proactive.

## Overview

In today’s fast‑evolving environment, organizations face a wide spectrum of risks. TrustMaven is designed to deliver a holistic risk management solution that brings together generalized risk management principles with specialized modules tailored to specific domains. Whether you need to manage financial exposures, cybersecurity threats, operational disruptions, or the emerging challenges of AI governance and algorithmic transparency, TrustMaven offers a scalable, adaptable framework to safeguard your organization.

---

## Features

- **Comprehensive Risk Identification & Assessment**
  - Capture risks from multiple domains such as:
    - **Financial Risk Management:** Credit, market, liquidity, and operational risks.
    - **Cybersecurity Risk Management:** Data breaches, cyberattacks, and IT vulnerabilities.
    - **Operational Risk Management:** Process failures, supply chain disruptions, and system breakdowns.
    - **Strategic Risk Management:** Business strategy, market shifts, and competitive pressures.
    - **Enterprise Risk Management (ERM):** A holistic view of all organizational risks.
    - **Regulatory and Compliance Risk Management:** Ensuring adherence to laws and internal policies.
    - **Reputational, Legal, and Political Risk Management:** Managing brand, legal liabilities, and geopolitical impacts.
    - **Data Privacy & Governance:** Protecting sensitive data and ensuring compliance with data protection laws.
    - **Innovation & Technology Risk Management:** Overseeing risks associated with emerging technologies.
    - **ESG Risk Management:** Addressing environmental, social, and governance risks.
    - **Model, ML/MLOps, & Algorithmic Risk Management:** Ensuring AI, machine learning, and algorithm-driven systems perform reliably, ethically, and transparently.
    - **Social Media & Public Relations Risk Management:** Mitigating risks related to online reputation.
    - **Investment Risk Management:** Evaluating risks within investment portfolios.
- **Risk Treatment & Mitigation Strategies**
  - Develop action plans to avoid, mitigate, transfer, or accept risks.
- **Continuous Monitoring & Reporting**
  - Real‑time dashboards and automated alerts for ongoing risk tracking.
- **Audit Trails & Documentation**
  - Maintain comprehensive logs and reports to support internal reviews and regulatory audits.

## Architecture

TrustMaven is built with a modular, scalable architecture to support diverse risk management needs:

- **Risk Repository:**  
  A centralized database that stores all risk data, including risk assessments, treatment plans, and audit logs.

- **Monitoring Engine:**  
  Real‑time dashboards that track risk metrics, alert users to changes, and provide visibility across all risk domains.

- **Analytics & Reporting Module:**  
  Tools for quantitative and qualitative risk analysis, enabling dynamic risk evaluation and comprehensive reporting.

- **Integration Layer:**  
  APIs and connectors for seamless integration with external systems (ERP, CRM, data sources) and third‑party tools.

- **User Interface:**  
  An intuitive, role‑based interface designed to provide clear access to risk management functions and actionable insights.

## Usage

Once the application is running, access the TrustMaven dashboard at `http://localhost:3000` (or your configured port). The dashboard provides:

- **Risk Registration:**  
  Add new risk entries across various categories.
- **Risk Assessment:**  
  Utilize built-in tools to analyze risk likelihood and impact.
- **Monitoring Dashboard:**  
  Visualize risk metrics, trends, and compliance status.
- **Reporting:**  
  Generate detailed reports for audits, internal reviews, and regulatory submissions.

For more detailed usage instructions, please refer to the [Documentation](docs).
